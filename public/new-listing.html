Shipping
How shipping works

We give customers free shipping, so your price needs to cover your postage (you've added this above). Add a postage amount that's a fair middle-ground for sending to both NZ and Australia from wherever you ship. On some orders you'll come out slightly ahead, on others slightly behind; it evens out.

ZIGGY's 15% commission comes off the total listed price (item + shipping), so factor that in. Always ship tracked, to both NZ and Australia — dispatch within 3 business days (or your stated made-to-order turnaround).
