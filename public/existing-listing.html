Shipping
How shipping works

Customers get free shipping, so your postage is baked into your price (you added it above). Pick a fair middle-ground amount that covers sending to both NZ and Australia from wherever you ship — some orders you'll be slightly ahead, others slightly behind, and it evens out.

Remember ZIGGY's 15% commission comes off the full listed price (item + shipping), so factor that in. Always ship tracked to both NZ and Australia, and dispatch within 3 business days (or your stated made-to-order turnaround).
