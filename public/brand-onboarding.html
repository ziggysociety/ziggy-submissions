The ZIGGY Five Criteria — tell us how you meet them

1. Independent — founder-led or small-team, no corporate ownership or mass-market retailers.

2. Ethical — genuine care for people, planet or community (sustainable materials, fair production, giving back, certified practices).

3. Quality — well-made products to a genuine craft standard (not necessarily premium).

4. Story — an authentic reason to exist, a clear point of view, something worth championing.

5. NZ based — local-first; for brands based and making in Aotearoa New Zealand (Australia coming soon).
